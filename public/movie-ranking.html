The best movies according to Tricia

My top 5 movies of all time

The Shawshank Redemption

This is the best Stephen King movie adaptation, and contains my favorite movie line: "Get busy living, or get busy dying"

Serenity

A wonderful conclusion to the short lived cult classic tv show Firefly

Avatar

A visually breathtaking epic action film.

Alien

This Ridley Scott classic was a game changer for thriller/horror movies

Child's Play

A campy horror film that became a cult classic.
